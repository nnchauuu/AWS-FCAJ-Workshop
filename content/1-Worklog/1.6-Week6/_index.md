### Week 6 Objectives:

* Learn about Amazon Relational Database Service (Amazon RDS).
* Understand the role of RDS in managing relational databases.
* Practice preparing the environment to connect EC2 with RDS.
* Create an RDS Database Instance and test the connection.
* Get familiar with backup, restore, and resource cleanup.

### Week 6 Results:

* Understood the role of Amazon RDS in managing relational databases.
* Learned key components such as DB Instance, DB Subnet Group, endpoint, and Security Group.
* Learned how to prepare the network environment to connect EC2 with RDS.
* Practiced creating an EC2 Instance and an RDS Database Instance.
* Tested the database connection through the endpoint.
* Understood the basics of backup, restore, DB Snapshot, and automated backup.
* Learned how to clean up resources to avoid unnecessary costs.

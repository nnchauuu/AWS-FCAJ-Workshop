### Week 6 Objectives:

* Learn about Amazon Relational Database Service (Amazon RDS).
* Understand the role of RDS in deploying and managing relational databases on AWS.
* Practice preparing a network environment to connect EC2 with RDS.
* Create an RDS Database Instance and test the connection from the application.
* Learn about backup, restore, and resource cleanup after practice.

### Week 7 Objectives:

* Learn how to deploy an application using Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling Group.
* Understand the role of Launch Template, Elastic Load Balancer, Target Group, and Auto Scaling Group.
* Practice scaling solutions such as manual scaling, scheduled scaling, dynamic scaling, and predictive scaling.
* Learn about Amazon CloudWatch for monitoring AWS resources and applications.
* Practice monitoring metrics, logs, alarms, and dashboards in CloudWatch.

This section records the technology events, workshops, and community days that I attended during my internship. These events gave me more opportunities to learn practical knowledge about Cloud, AI, DevOps, technology product development, teamwork skills, and career orientation in the field of Information Technology.

Through sharing sessions from speakers, mentors, and project teams, I not only had the chance to learn about new technologies but also understood more clearly how to apply knowledge in practice, from product development and system deployment to personal skill development.

### [Event 1 - FCAJ Community Day](4.1-Event1/)

This community event focused on technology product development, soft skills, startup stories, and practical applications of Cloud. The main topics included practical Hackathon experience shared by The Ballers Team, developing confidence and overcoming psychological barriers, the startup story of the Tu Vi Dai Viet project, analysis of the hidden iceberg in software project management and procrastination, as well as an introduction to Cloud learning support tools such as AWS Cloud Quest and Floci.

Through this event, I learned that a good technology product should start from real user needs. At the same time, it requires a combination of product thinking, teamwork skills, communication ability, and a proactive learning mindset. Besides knowledge about Cloud and technology, the event also helped me become more aware of the importance of confidence, emotional management, and the ability to overcome procrastination in both learning and work.

### [Event 2 - FCAJ Community Day](4.2-Event2/)

This community event focused on topics related to Cloud, DevOps, AI/ML, system security, container technology, and teamwork skills in the technology field. The main contents included applying Machine Learning with AWS WAF to detect and prevent cyberattacks, Docker and containerization in software development, the career development journey from IT Helpdesk to Sysadmin and Cloud/DevOps, building a multiplayer game system with AWS WebSockets, and principles for improving teamwork efficiency in technology projects.

Through this event, I gained a clearer understanding of the role of modern technologies in building and operating real-world systems. Machine Learning can help enhance security capabilities, Docker supports flexible application packaging and deployment, and WebSockets play an important role in systems that require real-time communication. In addition, the sharing sessions about career paths and teamwork skills also gave me a clearer direction for my personal development after the internship.
